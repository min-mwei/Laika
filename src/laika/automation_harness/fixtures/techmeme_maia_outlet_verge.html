Microsoft’s Maia 200 AI chip begins Azure rollout

Published Jan 26, 2026 • Hardware

Microsoft says its second‑generation Maia 200 accelerator is now live in the Azure US Central region, starting with internal AI workloads before expanding to select enterprise customers.

The company positions Maia 200 as an inference‑focused chip built on TSMC’s 3nm process, aimed at reducing token costs while keeping latency low for large model serving.

Key points

  • Rollout begins Jan 26, 2026 in Azure US Central.
  • Second‑generation Maia accelerator built on 3nm.
  • Focus on inference efficiency and internal AI services.
